The Paper Plane

To Make:

For this recipe, you will need a cocktail shaker, a jigger, a hawthorne strainer, ice, Bourbon, Amaro Nonino, Aperol, and freshly squeezed lemon juice. I like to serve mine in a frosted Nick and Nora glass.

Measure our 3/4 oz of Bourbon, Amaron Nonino, Aperol, and fresh lemon juice. Add to the cocktail shaker. Then add ice and shake vigoursly for 10-15 seconds.

Using a a hawthorne stainer and and additional small fine mesh stainer, double strain the cocktail into the frosted glass just removed from the freezer.

Enjoy this cocktail, cheers.
